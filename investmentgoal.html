Goal planner FAQ

Target mode vs project mode?
Target mode answers “how much SIP for this goal.” Project mode answers “what might this SIP grow into.”
Why grow the target with inflation?
₹50 lakh in 15 years usually buys less than ₹50 lakh today. Raising the target with inflation aims at similar buying power later.
What is the inflation-adjusted corpus?
The nominal end amount divided down by cumulative inflation. It is a rough view of real buying power.
Why only one return rate?
It keeps the model easy to follow. Use the three scenarios if you want to see higher or lower outcomes.

Practical tips

  • 🛟Build an emergency fund before you push SIPs hard.
  • 🎯Match how much equity you hold to your timeline. See risk & allocation.
  • 📶Raise SIPs when income rises (step-up in project mode).
  • 🏠For long goals, many people keep a simple diversified core. Read index vs active.
