📚 Goal planner FAQ

  • Target vs project? Target mode answers “how much SIP for a goal.” Project mode answers “what might this SIP become.”
  • Why grow the target with inflation? ₹50 lakh in 15 years buys less than ₹50 lakh today. Inflating the target aims at similar purchasing power.
  • What is the inflation-adjusted corpus? Nominal corpus divided by cumulative inflation — a rough view of real purchasing power.
  • Why one return rate? Keeps the model transparent. Use scenarios to stress-test higher or lower outcomes.

💡 Practical tips

  • Protect with an emergency fund before stretching SIPs.
  • Match equity share to your horizon — see risk & allocation.
  • Raise SIPs when income grows (step-up in project mode).
  • Pair long goals with simple cores like diversified funds — index vs active.
