📚 Investment Goal FAQs & Guidance

  • What is XIRR? XIRR (Extended Internal Rate of Return) gives your true annualized return, accounting for all cash flows and timing, making it the gold standard for comparing different investments.
  • Why consider inflation? Inflation reduces your money’s buying power over time. Always look at the inflation-adjusted corpus to see your real wealth growth.
  • What does “Future Corpus” mean? This is the total wealth you’ll accumulate, assuming all your planned investments and expected returns hold true.
  • What’s the benefit of investing monthly? Monthly SIPs smooth out market ups and downs, help you save consistently, and build wealth through compounding.

💡 Tips for Smart Investing

  • Start early—even small investments grow big over time.
  • Review your investments every year and rebalance if needed.
  • Increase your SIP amount as your income grows.
  • Consult a financial advisor for personalized advice.
