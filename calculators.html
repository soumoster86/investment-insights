How to use these tools

1. Use numbers you believe

Pick rates and tenures you find realistic. A small change in assumed return can move long-term results a lot.

2. Remember what is left out

Each tool is a simplified model. Taxes, fees, and real market ups and downs are often ignored unless the page says otherwise.

3. Read the matching guide

Open the related Learn page so the figure sits next to risk, liquidity, and who the product is for.
