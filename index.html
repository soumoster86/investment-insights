Why invest?

A one-minute primer — then use the dashboard above (goal snapshot, metals, currency) while you explore guides.

Wealth creation

Put money to work in assets that can grow faster than a savings account over long horizons.

Beat inflation

Returns that outpace inflation help protect purchasing power — model this in the goal planner and inflation tool.

Goal funding

House, education, retirement — map targets to SIPs, ladders, and timelines with transparent assumptions.
