Stocks

Stocks represent ownership shares in publicly traded companies. Investing in stocks allows you to participate in a company's growth and profits through capital appreciation and dividends. Stocks are suitable for investors seeking higher returns and willing to accept market volatility.

Mutual Funds

Mutual funds pool money from many investors to invest in diversified portfolios of stocks, bonds, or other securities. They are managed by professional fund managers and provide diversification and convenience. Mutual funds are ideal for investors looking for a balanced approach to growth and risk.

Learn more about Mutual Funds »

Fixed Deposits (FDs)

Fixed Deposits are safe, low-risk investment options offered by banks and financial institutions. You deposit a fixed amount for a specified tenure at a guaranteed interest rate. FDs provide steady returns and are suitable for conservative investors seeking capital protection.

Learn more about Fixed Deposits »

Cryptocurrencies

Cryptocurrencies are digital or virtual currencies that use cryptography for security and operate independently of central banks. They offer potential high returns but come with high risks.
