Why invest?

A one-minute primer. Then use the tools above (goal snapshot, metal rates, currency) while you browse the guides.

Grow your money

Over long periods, investments can grow faster than money left only in a savings account.

Keep up with prices

If returns beat inflation, your money keeps more of its buying power. Try the goal planner and inflation tool to see the idea.

Fund real goals

A house, education, or retirement. Use the goal planner, home-buy cost tool, SIPs, and deposit calculators with clear assumptions.
